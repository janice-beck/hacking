5.2: Writing Process

    Main Experiments

For this experiment, I’m making my thesis-writing process public, traceable, and live. Instead of treating writing as a private, polished outcome, I’m writing in a shared Google Doc — visible in real time to anyone with the link. This is an attempt to hack the academic process: exposing (myself), making public what’s usually hidden. Also, resisting the urge to only show a polished result, to have total control and authority of my output. Writing becomes transparent, vulnerable, open to interruption. .
