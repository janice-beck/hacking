2.2: Default Settings

    Main Experiments

A script that changes the default settings with every new document. Page dimensions, margins, font, font size, and predefined colors are randomized. The experiment began with frustration at how layout software imposes defaults — Minion Pro, 12 pt, 12.7 mm margins. These presets aren’t neutral; they encode assumptions about legibility, hierarchy, and aesthetics. Automating their disruption became a way to question and surface those assumptions.
