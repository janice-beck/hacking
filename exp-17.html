5: How did it feel?

    Main Experiments

From this point on (21.05.), I started including a section about how I felt during each experiment. Designers often have a complicated relationship with emotion. There’s a lot beneath the surface — not just insecurity or self-doubt, but also pride, excitement, even moments that feel almost manic. And yet, we rarely speak about it.
Maybe this, too, is a form of hacking: turning inward, making the emotional process visible. Even admitting these feelings can feel ridiculous — like right now.
